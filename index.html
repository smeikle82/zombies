Zombie Outbreak Simulation v0.2

Welcome to the Zombie Outbreak Simulation! Inspired by Conway's Game of Life, watch the chaos unfold.

Controls:

  • Reset: Clears the simulation and starts fresh.
  • Pause/Resume: Stops or continues the simulation clock.
  • -/+: Decreases or increases the simulation speed (Ticks Per Second).

Current Features:

Humans (white) try to avoid Zombies (green)! More features coming soon.

Speed: 2 TPS
